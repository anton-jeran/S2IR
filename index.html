SPEECH2IR-GAN: ROOM IMPULSE RESPONSE ESTIMATOR FROM REVERBERANT SPEECH SIGNAL

Authors: Anton Ratnarajah, Ishwarya Ananthabhotla, Vamsi Krishna Ithapu, Pablo Hoffmann, Dinesh Manocha and Paul Calamia

Abstract:We propose a GAN-based neural network (SPEECH2IR-GAN) to estimate the time domain room impulse response from the reverberant speech. Our SPEECH2IR-GAN network encodes RIR features from the reverberant speech and constructs RIR from the encoded features. We train our network on energy decay relief loss to capture energy-based acoustic properties of reverberant speech. We show that the estimated RIRs using our SPEECH2IR-GAN have similar energy distribution as the ground truth RIR. Our proposed approach outperforms the state-of-the-art RIR estimator in acoustic metrics such as energy decay relief loss and early reflection energy loss by 72\% and 22\% respectively. Estimated RIRs from our SPEECH2IR-GAN gives the least mean square error when compared to the state-of-the-art method. We estimate RIRs from real-world far-field speech from the AMI corpus and show the benefit of estimated RIRs in a Kaldi automatic speech recognition task.
